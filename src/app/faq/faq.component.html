Information & FAQ

  • What is the KEF?

    The Krytan Expeditionary Force is a military styled roleplay guild dedicated to Queen Jennah and the whole of Kryta. Our main base of operations is the gilded hollow, however when we are in the greater world our main base is at Nebo Terrace in Gendarren fields.

  • What can I expect on a roleplay side of things?

    We are not a hardcore roleplay guild. We do not enforce correct grammar/spelling, and we do not only take veterans to roleplay. Many of our long standing members first got into roleplay upon joining the KEF, so we very warmly welcome players new to roleplay, and we would love to help you get more acquainted with it.

    Being military styled, you can expect our inner guild interactions to be fairly, well… military styled. We have a rank system with many different ranks from many different militaries around the real world. We salute a lot, and address our superiors as “sir” or “ma’am”. Many of our open world events and roleplay arcs involve combat of some sort (whether IC combat or combat gameplay in the open world).

      Because this guild is themed towards a military group associated and supported by the Krytan crown, it is expected that all characters entering the guild have been interviewed and have passed the following criteria:
    • That their criminal history is clear or limited to petty crimes, resulting in no serious conflict with Tyria’s various governing bodies, including war crimes with the PACT.
    • That they are unaffiliated with factions rivaling the Krytan crown.
    • That they do not join the guild with the intent to destroy, undermine, or otherwise harm the KEF.

    You may elect to either have this interview with or without a proper roleplay session, so long as the character passes the previous points. If your character conflicts with any of those points, they must be interviewed in-character or cleared with Elivaras#3544, Hapless Peasant#8681, or Scriberat#7264 on discord. If you would like an interview, please contact one of the previously mentioned tags or FoxxyPoison#9383.

    Being an open world guild, we don’t only hang around in the guild hall or cities and just derp around. We spend a lot of time in the open world, doing events, defending settlements, etc while roleplaying as we go. While we do have events where there is no open world involved, if you want to get involved with us, expect that we will do a lot in the open world. There are plenty of guilds to join if you want to hang out at taverns 24/7 -- we are not one of them.

  • Are there any uniforms or rep requirements?

    Being a military styled roleplay guild, you would think yes- but no. We try not to force people into following too many strict guidelines, so there are no uniforms. If you do want to display the KEF colours, you can dye your armour red/white/black.

    There are no rep requirements, although we appreciate it if you represent us during our meetings.

  • What is the policy for meetings and attendance?

    We meet four times a week, but note that these meetings are not mandatory. Each member is encouraged to try and join us for as many as possible, but we do not have a “minimum number of meetings you have to attend per week” kind of deal.

    Monday and Wednesday night meetings start at 7:20 PM EDT, and are led by members of the KEF who submit leading applications. The leader gets to choose what to do, from fractals, structured story, map metas, patrols, etc.

    Friday night meetings start at 9:20 PM EST, and are open world patrol/recruitment nights or Side-Arc nights. For more information about our Side-Arcs, click here. For an Open-World Patrol, we partake in an IC patrol through low levelled zones with the intent of chilling and having a good time alongside bringing new members into the fold. We want to be very open and encouraging to anyone who stops by to join us. For a Side-Arc, we follow the plot of a story arc created by one of our very own guild members, which can span several Fridays.

    Note: You can apply to lead on a Friday, but we would prefer to keep it to a patrol (if it's not a side-arc night).

    Saturday night meetings start at 9:20 PM EST, and are our roleplay arc nights. We have a dedicated writer’s team developing story arcs for the guild, to bring some deeper roleplay and plot to our members. These are our most roleplay oriented and most important meetings, so if you are able to make these meetings, it would be much appreciated.

    Note: If you weren't around for our past arcs, you can read the summaries of them in the resources section.
