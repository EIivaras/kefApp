The Siege of Garenhoff

All across Tyria, Joko's forces have been using captured inquest asura gates to transport troops to the continent. Upon arrival, the hordes of awakened split in all directions, capturing important landmarks and securing targets of military importance.

Tipped off by a sunspear informant, the Krytan Expeditionary Force, led by Commander Physicist Fizz and serving under Queen Jennah of Kryta, gains important intel detailing an attack that Palawa Joko has planned on the coastal city of Garenhoff.

While small, the port town is a crucial point of supply for Kessex, and if it falls, Joko will have no issues keeping an army in the region. Forces must be moved to intercept immediately, and keep Joko's awakened from establishing a beach-head!

Join the Krytan Expeditionary Force as they move to the defence of Garenhoff, and join the fight to keep the awakened horde off Krytan shores!
