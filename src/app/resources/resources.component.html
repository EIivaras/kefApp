Resources

Here you will find all the resources you might need, from combat rules and guidelines, to story arc summaries, to a villain sign up sheet- you name it!

  • ROSTER. Please use this document to add in your account and character information associated with this guild.

  • COMBAT. For rules used in formal RP combat during major guild events.

  • ARC SUMMARIES. If you were unable to attend a story arc meeting or simply wish to read along with our progress.

  • ARC SUBMISSIONS. If you would like to submit an idea for an upcoming arc!

  • VILLAINS. If you're interested in becoming a villain in one of the guild's RP events.

  • WRITERS. If you'd like to apply to become a part of the writing team that deals with the guild's major arcs on Saturdays, please submit your application via this form!

  • LEADER APP. If you'd like to apply to lead one of the meetings, this is the form for you!
