KEF Affairs

Here, you'll find a list of documents relating to the In-Character Internal Affairs of the KEF. This includes the following:

Guild Hall Overview: Contains an overview of the security, amenities, and resource situation of the guild (is subject to change based on need). KEF Charter: Contains the charter of the Force, outlining its mission statement, structure and procedure, and authority. This includes the updates to the Charter after revisal by the Ministry during the approval process. Member Agreement: Contains the member agreement that all characters have been implied to have agreed to upon joining the KEF; including Oaths and some of the larger member rules. Discipline Policy: Outlines the IC rules, punishments, and procedures of the Krytan Expeditionary Force regarding internal policy (for a more concise overview of the rules see the Member Agreement). Officers and Teams: A listing of all of the various officer positions and teams within the KEF. Golems: Contains descriptions of the types of Golems in the Krytan Expeditionary Force.
